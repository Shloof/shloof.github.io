App Concept & UX Design

Mix & Drinx

Mix & Drinx is a user-centered cocktail app for flavor experimenters. It delivers customizable bottled cocktails straight to your door, perfect for hangouts, celebrations, or movie nights.
Role
UX & UI
Micro-copy
Branding
Tools
Figma
After Effects
Illustrator

Goal

To create an app that makes custom-made cocktails accessible at home, enabling social gatherings and celebrations without the need to go out to crowded or expensive bars.

The Challenges

Designing a seamless, user-friendly experience that captures the joy of social drinking in a home setting, especially in times of social restrictions like Covid-19.
Personalization
Offering flavor experimentation without overwhelming the user
Usability & Fun
keeping the interface intuitive while maintaining a playful brand feel
Social Element
Making the app feel like a shared experience, not just an order

User Flow

Wireframes

Birthday Verification
Instead of a simple yes/no age check, users enter their date of birth. This makes verification clearer and more engaging, while also allowing underage users to be directed to non-alcoholic options.
Understanding the user
During sign-up, the app learns about the user by identifying allergies and capturing taste preferences to personalize the experience.
Making it engaging
Early wireframes outlined the cocktail creation steps. They highlighted the need for ingredient visuals and better navigation.
Order review
A structured layout lets users easily check their chosen ingredients, customize details, and confirm the final cocktail before adding it to the cart.

Design

Onboarding

Onboarding

Date of birth
The user begins by entering their date of birth. If they are under 18, a message appears informing them that they can only order Mocktails (non-alcoholic drinks).
Personalization
The user can mark any allergies or skip the step if none apply. Choosing a preferred flavor helps make the flow more personal and engaging.
Onboarding completion
The user finishes the journey with a smile, cheering on a yummy animation cocktail.

Creating a Cocktail

Creating a Cocktail

Home
After the onboarding, the user lands on the Home screen, where they can start creating their own cocktail or choose from a variety of existing options to generate a surprising mix.
Cocktail or Non-Alcoholic
After the onboarding, the user lands on the Home screen, where they can start creating their own cocktail or choose from a variety of existing options to generate a surprising mix.
Drink size options
The cocktail ordering process includes five steps with a progress bar that also allows going back to edit. The first step is choosing a bottle size that animates based on the user's selection.
Choosing flavors & ingredients
In the following steps, the user selects the alcohol base, liqueur, and fruits, with pairing recommendations and an animated carousel for smooth transitions between ingredients.
Shake Interaction
In the final step, the user performs a shake gesture to mix their cocktail, adding a delightful UX moment that feels both playful and interactive.

Customization

Customization

Customized bottle
If the user wishes, they can personalize their bottle by adding and editing text - adjusting the font, color, and size.
Design Options
They can add their own design or choose from a variety of existing patterns.
Order Tracking
At the end of the process, the user reaches a tracking screen with delivery time and order status. While waiting, they can explore other users' cocktails for inspiration.

Conclusions

Mix & Drinx brings the cocktail bar experience home with a user-friendly app that celebrates flavor experimentation.
  1. Recommendations: helping users discover new and personalized flavor combinations.
  2. Social Integration: Making cocktail creation a shared and social experience.
  3. Seamless Ordering: From easy customization to smooth doorstep delivery.
