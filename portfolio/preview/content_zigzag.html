Onboarding

Onboarding

Date of birth
The user begins by entering their date of birth. If they are under 18, a message appears informing them that they can only order Mocktails (non-alcoholic drinks).
Personalization
The user can mark any allergies or skip the step if none apply. Choosing a preferred flavor helps make the flow more personal and engaging.
Onboarding completion
The user finishes the journey with a smile, cheering on a yummy animation cocktail.

Creating a Cocktail

Creating a Cocktail

Home
After the onboarding, the user lands on the Home screen, where they can start creating their own cocktail or choose from a variety of existing options to generate a surprising mix.
Cocktail or Non-Alcoholic
After the onboarding, the user lands on the Home screen, where they can start creating their own cocktail or choose from a variety of existing options to generate a surprising mix.
Drink size options
The cocktail ordering process includes five steps with a progress bar that also allows going back to edit. The first step is choosing a bottle size that animates based on the user's selection.
Choosing flavors & ingredients
In the following steps, the user selects the alcohol base, liqueur, and fruits, with pairing recommendations and an animated carousel for smooth transitions between ingredients.
Shake Interaction
In the final step, the user performs a shake gesture to mix their cocktail, adding a delightful UX moment that feels both playful and interactive.

Customization

Customization

Customized bottle
If the user wishes, they can personalize their bottle by adding and editing text - adjusting the font, color, and size.
Design Options
They can add their own design or choose from a variety of existing patterns.
Order Tracking
At the end of the process, the user reaches a tracking screen with delivery time and order status. While waiting, they can explore other users' cocktails for inspiration.
